About Me

My research expertise is in experimental fluid mechanics regarding the liquid-gas / fluid-solid interaction through experiments together with theoretical approaches and numerical tools. Using optimizied experimental design and multidisciplinary collaboration, I have contributed a variety of research fields such as spray process, droplet production, flow measurement technique, biology, animal behavior, surgical devices, digital twin as well as fundamental fluid mechanics.

  • Go to Research Areas

I am a postdoctor at Georgia Institute of Technology working with Professor Saad Bhamla. I earned my Ph.D. degree in the department of mechanical engineering at Seoul National University in 2022 under the supervision of professor Hyungmin Park. In the same department, I received M.S., and B.S. degrees in 2018, and 2015, respectively. I received the best Ph.D. thesis award at Seoul National Univerity.
